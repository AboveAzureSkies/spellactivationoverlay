Spell Activation Overlay

This WoW Classic addon mimics Spell Activation Overlays, formerly known as Spell Alerts, which were introduced in Cataclysm.

For example, Frost Death Knights can have procs for Rime and Killing Machine, while Enhancement Shamans have stacks of Maelstrom Weapon.

There is nothing to configure. Just install the addon and enjoy your procs!

 

Death Knight: Killing Machine, Rime

 

Mage: Missile Barrage, Brain Freeze, Fingers of Frost, Hot Streak, Heating Up (experimental)

     

 

Paladin: Art of War

 

Priest: Surge of Light, Serendipity

 

Shaman: Maelstrom Weapon

 

Warrior: Bloodsurge, Sudden Death, Sword and Board

   

 

Contribution

The addon is very new.

Classes are implemented one after the other, and I don’t pretend to know all effects of all classes anyway.

If you wish to contribute, please file an Issue or send a Pull Request to the github repository.
